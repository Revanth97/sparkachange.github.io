Spitha 2

Where your talent will make a difference.

The Sequel To Our Maiden Event

Spitha the talent show provided performers with a stage to show off their talent,an audience with an evening overflowing with entertainment, and the students of a blind school the resources they needed. As Spitha was an incredible success, the Spark A Change team has no choice but to bring it back into the light. Spitha 2 will be a bigger and better event that its previous iteration, and we can't wait to bring it to you!

More details about Spitha 2 will be launched shortly, be sure to keep a close eye on our website and Facebook page for updates!
