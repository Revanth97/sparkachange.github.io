Spítha II

Spitha the talent show provided performers with a stage to show off their talent, an audience with an evening overflowing with entertainment, and the students of a blind school the resources they needed. As Spitha was an incredible success, we at Spark A Change have no choice but to bring it back into the light. Spitha II will be a grander event than its predecessor, and we can't wait to bring it to you!

More details about Spitha II will be released shortly, be sure to keep a close eye on this space for updates!
